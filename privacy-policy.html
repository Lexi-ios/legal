Lexi Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 5, 2026  |  Last Updated: March 5, 2026

Our Core Commitment: Lexi is an offline OCR tool designed with privacy at its core. Your images and recognized text are processed entirely on your device and are never uploaded to any external server. We respect and protect your privacy.

1. Introduction

Lexi (hereinafter referred to as "the App") is developed and operated by the Lexi development team (hereinafter referred to as "we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully before using the App. By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information We Do NOT Collect

For clarity, we do not collect the following information:

  • Images you capture or select from your photo library
  • Text content recognized by OCR
  • Personal identity information such as your name, phone number, or email address
  • Your precise geographic location
  • Your contacts, messages, or call history
  • Usage data from other apps on your device

2.2 Information Processed Locally on Your Device

The following information is processed and stored only on your local device and is never transmitted to our servers:

Information Type Purpose Storage Method
Captured/selected images OCR text recognition Local App sandbox directory
Recognized text results Display, editing, copying, and sharing Local database
History recognition records Record management and history review Local database
App preferences and settings Saving your personalized settings Local storage
Coin balance and purchase records Managing virtual currency and unlocked features Local storage

2.3 Information That May Be Transmitted

The following information may be transmitted to third-party services under specific circumstances:

Information Type Recipient Purpose
Purchase transaction data Apple (App Store) Processing in-app purchases (Coin purchases)
Crash logs and anonymous usage statistics Firebase (Google) Improving app stability and user experience

3. Device Permissions

The App may request the following device permissions, all of which are necessary for core functionality:

Permission Purpose Required?
Camera Capturing photos for OCR text recognition Required for camera feature; not needed for album feature
Photo Library Selecting existing images for recognition (uses PHPicker, which only accesses photos you select — no full library access required) Required for album feature

You can manage and revoke these permissions at any time through your iOS device's Settings. Revoking permissions may prevent related features from functioning properly.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

  • All recognized images and text data are stored within the iOS app sandbox directory, protected by iOS system-level security.
  • Other apps cannot access data within this App's sandbox.
  • History records are stored locally on-device using a SQLite database.

4.2 Data Deletion

  • You can delete history recognition records individually or in bulk within the App.
  • Uninstalling the App will automatically erase all locally stored data.
  • Deleted data cannot be recovered. Please confirm before deleting.

4.3 Security Measures

  • The App relies on iOS native security mechanisms (sandbox isolation, data protection) to safeguard your data.
  • OCR recognition is performed entirely on-device using the Apple Vision framework, with no network transmission involved.
  • In-app purchases are processed through Apple's StoreKit security framework. We never access any payment information.

5. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services, each with its own privacy policy:

5.1 Apple Services

  • App Store / StoreKit: Processes in-app purchases. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details.
  • Vision Framework: Apple's native OCR engine, which runs entirely on-device and does not transmit any data.

5.2 Firebase (Google)

  • Firebase Analytics: Collects anonymized app usage statistics to help us understand usage patterns and improve the product.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Collects crash logs to help us fix issues and improve app stability.
  • All data collected by Firebase is anonymized and does not include your images, recognized text, or other personal content.
  • For more details, please refer to Firebase's Privacy Policy.

6. Children's Privacy

This App is suitable for users of all ages (App Store age rating: 4+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The App does not require user registration or the provision of any personal identity information.

7. Your Rights

As a user, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You can view your history recognition records and settings preferences within the App at any time.
  • Right to Deletion: You can delete any history records within the App at any time, or erase all data by uninstalling the App.
  • Right to Control Permissions: You can manage the App's device permissions at any time through your iOS system Settings.
  • Right to Withdraw: You can stop using the App and uninstall it at any time.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for the latest information on our privacy practices. Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
